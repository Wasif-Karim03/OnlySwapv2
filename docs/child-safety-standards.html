Child Safety Standards

OnlySwap is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

1. Age Restrictions

  • OnlySwap is not intended for users under 13 years of age
  • Users must be college students with verified .edu email addresses
  • Age verification is required during account registration
  • Users under 18 must have permission from a parent or guardian

2. Content Moderation

  • All user-generated content is monitored for inappropriate material
  • Automated systems flag potentially harmful content
  • Users can report inappropriate content or behavior
  • Reported content is reviewed and removed if it violates our policies

3. Reporting Mechanisms

  • In-app reporting system for users to report safety concerns
  • Direct email contact for urgent safety issues
  • All reports are reviewed within 24 hours
  • Serious violations are reported to appropriate authorities

4. User Safety Features

  • User blocking functionality
  • Content reporting system
  • Account verification requirements
  • Community guidelines enforcement

5. Compliance and Reporting

OnlySwap complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Regional and national child protection laws
  • Platform policies for child safety

We report suspected child sexual abuse material (CSAM) to:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Appropriate law enforcement authorities
  • Relevant regulatory bodies

7. Prevention Measures

  • Educational content about online safety
  • Clear community guidelines
  • Regular platform monitoring
  • User education on reporting mechanisms
