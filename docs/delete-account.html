Delete Your OnlySwap Account

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your OnlySwap account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the OnlySwap app on your device
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to the "Danger Zone" section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly.

Contact Us to Delete Your Account

Email us at:

onlyswapwck@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your account email address
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account information (name, email, profile)
  • Your product listings
  • Your bids
  • Your chat messages
  • Your profile picture
  • All associated user data

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or compliance purposes:

  • Support tickets or help requests (for compliance records)
  • Data required by law enforcement (if applicable)

All personal data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all your listings, bids, and messages
  • If you have active transactions, please complete them before deleting your account
  • You can create a new account later if you change your mind
